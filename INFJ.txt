Professions suited to this archetype: Psychologist, Counselor, Social Worker, Mediator, Minister, Human Resources, Therapist

Notable INFJs: Dr. Martin Luther King, Thomas Jefferson, Mahatma Gandhi, Niels Bohr, Noam Chomsky, George Harrison, Carl Jung, Agatha Christie
