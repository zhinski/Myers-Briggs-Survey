ISTJ - The Realist: Quiet, serious, earn success by thoroughness and dependability. Practical, matter-of-fact, realistic, and responsible. Decide logically what should be done and work toward it steadily, regardless of distractions. Take pleasure in making everything orderly and organized - their work, their home, their life. Value traditions and loyalty.

Professions suited to this archetype: Accountant, Government, Military Officer, Auditor, Business Analyst, Physician, Librarian

Notable ISTJs: Jeff Bezos, Robert DeNiro, Natalie Portman, Sigmund Freud, Dwight D. Eisenhower, Henry Ford, Angela Merkel, Queen Elizabeth, Jackie Joyner-Kersee
