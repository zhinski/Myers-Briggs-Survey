INTP - The Scientist: Seek to develop logical explanations for everything that interests them. Theoretical and abstract, interested more in ideas than in social interaction. Quiet, contained, flexible, and adaptable. Have unusual ability to focus in depth to solve problems in their area of interest. Skeptical, sometimes critical, always analytical.

Professions suited to this archetype: College Professor, Mathematician, Financial Analyst, Chemist, Lawyer, Judge, Researcher, Physicist

Notable INTPs: Albert Einstein, Tina Fey, Meryl Streep, Abraham Lincoln, Marie Curie, Jane Austen, Charles Darwin, Rene Descartes
